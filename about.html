We have three cats named Yoshi, Marcella, and Shelley. Yoshi is the orange cat whos about 18 years old. I grew up with him! Shelley is the black and gray cat who is about 6 years old. We found her meowing her lungs out sitting on top of our neighbors tire. She is the loudest cat in the group be far. Marcella is the black cat and she is the youngest. I used to work at a greenhouse and she was found in the woods there. So I brought her home with me.

Ernest Hemmingway said "One cat just leads to another."

That is true in my familys case. Cats are independent with a mind of their own. I have grown up with cats my whole life. They all have very different personalities.
